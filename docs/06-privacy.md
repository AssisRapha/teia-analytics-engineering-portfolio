# Privacy & publication notes

This is a public portfolio repository.

## What is real vs what is public
- The **data model and governance approach** reflect a real operational system used in production.
- The **data in `sample-data/` is fictional** and exists only to demonstrate relationships and BI-friendly structure.

## What is NOT published
This repository does not include:
- real client names or identifiers
- session narratives, clinical notes, or report text
- meeting links, calendars, emails, transcripts, or recordings
- real dates/times that could identify a person
- any sensitive or personal information

## Screenshots policy (if `images/` exists)
Any screenshots included must be either:
- fully based on fictional demo data, or
- anonymized/blurred so that no client-identifying content is visible

## Purpose
The purpose of this repo is to demonstrate:
- data modeling (Cases / Sessions / Protocols + bridge table)
- capture governance and controlled vocabularies
- traceability and dashboard design

Not to publish TEIA client content or confidential materials.
